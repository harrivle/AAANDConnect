About the App

What is the purpose of the AAA Connect App?

The AAA Connect web app serves to automate the pairing of undergraduate students in AAA with a mentor who relates to their career interests.

How does the App work?

AAA first seeks out alumni who are interested in mentoring undergraduate students. Then, students who sign-up for AAA Connect will be able to indicate parameters to find a mentor. These parameters include: Major, Location, and Current Job/Career.
